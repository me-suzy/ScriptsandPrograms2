Send Email Service

You can use this facility to send email to members that register the services. You can use macros on your email content (See below notes) . To ensure the email content, click "Email Output Test", this button will generate example of email content on the end of this page.

Save email content as template.

Notes: 

  • The macros you can use on the email content are: {~hp_name}, {~hp_url}, {~hp_slogan}, {~member_name}, {~member_fullname}, {~svc_subject}, {~svc_name}, dan {~svc_desc} 
  • After click "Send Now", you can break the connection to server. Email sending will continue.
