As you can see, WEBK build everything from simple files and folders, this is the list of
features currently supported by WEBK:
<br><br>
<b>
<li>Thumbnails generation for text files, source codes, images
<li>Custom download handling for text files, source codes and images
<li>Locked folders with plain passwords or MD5 hashes
<li>Support for custom actions: HTML, NOTE, NEWS and UPLOAD
<li>Automatic hits counter for each individual item
<li>Simple modules system to enhance WEBK
<li>Theme support
</b>
<br><br>
More features to come (if you want, you can contribute, see support section)
