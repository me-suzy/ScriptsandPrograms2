<b style="color:blue"><u>Theming support:</u><br></b>
<br>
To create your own theme, you need to follow simple steps:
<br><br>
<b>
<li>1) Edit webk/include/config.inc.php and change the value of WEBK_THEME for the name of the theme you want.
<li>2) Duplicate folder: webk/template/default to webk/template/your_theme_name
<li>3) Duplicate folder: htdocs/css/default to htdocs/css/your_theme_name
<li>4) Duplicate folder: htdocs/images/default to htdocs/images/your_theme_name
</b><br><br>
If you want to create custom themes, feel free to send it to me, I will add it to a future version of WEBK.
