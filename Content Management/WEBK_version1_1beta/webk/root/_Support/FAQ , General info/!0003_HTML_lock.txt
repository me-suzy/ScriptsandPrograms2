<b style="color:blue"><u>Locking a folder:</u><br></b>
<br>
To lock a folder (and subfolders) with a password, you need to create a filename named ".locked" in the ".meta" subfolder of the desired folder.<br><br>
The file will need to contain a single line:<br><br>
<b>PLAIN=plainpassword<br></b>
OR<br>
<b>MD5=saltless_MD5_HASH_go_here<br><br></b>
