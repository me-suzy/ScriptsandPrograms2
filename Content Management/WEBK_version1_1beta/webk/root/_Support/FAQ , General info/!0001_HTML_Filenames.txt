<b style="color:blue"><u>Filenames:</u><br></b>
<br>
<b>
<li>A filename starting with a "." is hidden/ignored
<li>A filename starting with a "_" is interpreted as a menu item (only in the root folder)
<li>A filename starting with a "!" is interpreted as a custom action (Vertical align: TOP)
<li>A filename starting with a "@" is interpreted as a custom action (Vertical align: BOTTOM)
<br><br></b>
