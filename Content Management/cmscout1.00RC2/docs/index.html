CMScout

You might be asking yourself, "What is CMScout?"

CMScout is a CMS (Content management system) for scouting related groups from around the world. A CMS is a piece of web software that makes it easy for you to install, and manage a website. CMScout includes all the features of other major CMS's that are available (Like PHP-Nuke, Mambo, e107, etc.).

Why should you use CMScout for your scout troop, when there are all those other choices available? Simply because CMScout is designed from the ground up to be used with scouting. It includes "Group Sites" which allows each and every patrol to have their own personalised website, scouts can also upload photos and write articles for the website,

CMScout also includes a easy to use "Award" badge system that allows you to define award badges and requirements, and keep track of what items members of your troop have completed. You can even have different Award scheme's which will allow you to, for example, have a your Cub Pack, and Scout Group on the same website, but give Cubs the "Cub" award scheme, and Scouts, the "Scout" award scheme.
