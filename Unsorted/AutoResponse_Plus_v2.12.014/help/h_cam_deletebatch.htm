DELETING MULTIPLE SUBSCRIBERS

Overview

  1. Click on Subscribers on the menu bar.
  2. Set the filter for the subscribers you want to delete (or click Clear Filter on the sub menu bar for all subscribers).
  3. Click on Batch Delete on the sub menu bar.
  4. On the confirmation screen, click the Confirm Delete button.

When you have clicked the Confirm Delete button please wait for confirmation that the process is complete before taking any further action. This could take a few minutes for long lists.
