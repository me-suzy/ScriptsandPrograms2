INSERTING 3RD PARTY ADS IN YOUR MESSAGES

Autoresponse Plus has a facility for allowing you to insert ads in your follow-up and one-off messages. If you run an e-zine or newsletter, you can generate revenue by selling ad space.

The system supports up to 3 ads. These are defined in the System Setup screen. Click Setup on the menu bar to go there. The fields you need to enter are:

  • Advert 1
  • Advert 2
  • Advert 3

The ads may include HTML code if they will be inserted in HTML messages.

Ads can be included in messages by inserting the tags

  • {AD 1}
  • {AD 2}
  • {AD 3}

These are case sensitive and should be entered in capital letters.

EXAMPLE MESSAGE
Before today's featured article, let's take a commercial break.

{AD 1}

Today's featured article is about smart autoresponders and how you can use them to boost your sales ten-fold.
