DELETING A SINGLE SUBSCRIBER

Overview

  1. Click on Subscribers on the menu bar.
  2. Find the subscriber you want to delete (perhaps using filtering).
  3. Click on Delete beside the campaign you want to delete.

When you click on Delete (Step 3), you will be asked to confirm your choice. Click on the Confirm Delete button to confirm that you want to permanently remove this subscriber.

Deleting a subscriber also deletes the current follow-up sequence for that subscriber.
