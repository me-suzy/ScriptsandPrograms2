HOW DO PEOPLE UNSUBSCRIBE FROM MY AUTORESPONDERS?

There are two ways that contacts can unsubscribe from an autoresponder:

  • Click the unsubscribe link at the end of a message.
  • Send a blank e-mail to your autoresponder's subscription address with the word 'unsubscribe' in the subject line.
  • Click the unsubscribe link at the end of a message

You need to have chosen to include unsubscribe links in the autoresponder's messages. This is done in the autoresponder's page layout screen.

On unsubscribing, the contact will be shown a confirmation web page and they will receive no more follow-up messages. The campaign will be flagged as 'User Cancelled' in the subscriber database. In the System Setup screen you can also set whether a confirmation e-mail will be sent. The text for this can also be entered there.

  • Send a blank e-mail to your autoresponder's listening address with the word unsubscribe as the subject

This e-mail needs to be sent from the e-mail address that was used to subscribe to the autoresponder. The follow-up sequence/subscriber will be flagged as 'User Cancelled' in the subscriber database. The contact will receive no more follow-up messages. The contact will receive confirmation by e-mail, only if the option for this is set in the System Setup screen.
