TESTING AN AUTORESPONDER

Overview

  1. Click on Autoresponders on the menu bar.
  2. Click on Test beside the autoresponder to be tested.
  3. A confirmation screen will ask you to confirm your choice.

Testing allows you to quickly check that follow-up messages are formatted correctly.

The test features instantly sends all the plain text and HTML follow-up messages to the e-mail address you entered in the Your Profile screen. You can enter your e-mail address by clicking the Your Profile link on the menu bar.

Using the test feature is better than subscribing to the autoresponder. This could take days or weeks to complete depending on the message intervals you have set.
