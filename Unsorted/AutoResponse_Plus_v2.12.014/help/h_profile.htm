CHANGING YOUR PROFILE AND MY RECORD

Overview

  1. Click on Your Profile on the menu bar.
  2. Change your profile entries as required.
  3. Click the Save Changes button.

When you click on Your Profile (Step 1), you will see the Your Profile screen.

  • Owner name

This is the name you use to login to Autoresponse Plus. The default is 'owner' and this should be changed immediately after installation.

  • Password

This is the password you use to login to Autoresponse Plus. The default is '12345' and this should be changed immediately after installation.

  • Confirm password

If you change the password, you need to confirm the change here.

  • Your e-mail address

Autoresponse Plus will use this address to send you e-mails generated by the system (eg when testing your autoresponders). This address is never inserted in follow-up messages nor displayed in confirmation web pages.

  • Affiliate nickname

If you have joined the Autoresponse Plus affiliate program, you need to enter your affiliate nickname here.

  • My Record

These are entries which you can include in your messages, headers, footer and subjects using the tags shown. The My Record tags make it easy to change contact and other details without having to individually edit every follow-up message on your system.

EXAMPLE MESSAGE BODY TEXT
You can call me direct at any time, my phone number is {MY_PHONE1}.

WHAT YOUR SUBSCRIBER SEES
You can call me direct at any time, my phone number is 123-456-7890.

When you have changed the profile details as you require, click the Save Changes button.
