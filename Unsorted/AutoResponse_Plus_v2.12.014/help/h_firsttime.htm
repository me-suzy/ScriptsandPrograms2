LOGGING IN

You can reach the Autoresponse Plus login screen in your browser by going to the URL
http://www.yourdomain.com/cgi-bin/arplus/arplus.pl

After installation, the default Owner Name is 'owner' and the default Password is '12345'. You should change this immediately by clicking on the Your Profile link on the menu bar.

Immediately after installation you may get a warning message saying that the setup procedure is not complete. Details on this can be found in the file 'install.htm' supplied with the software.
