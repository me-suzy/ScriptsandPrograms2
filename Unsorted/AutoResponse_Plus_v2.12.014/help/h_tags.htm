DYNAMIC CONTENT - COMPLETE LIST OF TAGS

Autoresponse Plus has many dynamic content tags that you can insert into messages. These will be replaced by 'live' data just before the message is sent. Dynamic content tags can also be inserted in:

  • message subjects
  • plain or HTML message body text
  • headers and footers
  • one-off mailings

PERSONALIZATION
{FIRSTNAME default} Your contact's first name
{LASTNAME default} Your contact's last name
{FULLNAME default} Your contact's full name
{EMAIL default} Your contact's e-mail address
{AUTORESPONDER default} The subscription address of the autoresponder your contact subscribed to
{TRACKINGTAG default} The tracking tag associated with your contact's subscription
{UNSUBSCRIBE_LINK} The URL that will unsubscribe your contact
{UNSUBSCRIBE_LINK_TEXT} The unsubscribe text as defined in the System Setup screen

DATES
{SHORTDATE offset} Date in the format 3 Dec 2000 offset by the number of days specified.
{LONGDATE offset} Date in the format Sunday, 3 December 2000 offset by the number of days specified.
{USDATE offset} Date in the format mm/dd/yyyy offset by the number of days specified.
{EUROPEANDATE offset} Date in the format dd/mm/yyyy offset by the number of days specified.

MY RECORD
{MY_TITLE} Your title (eg Mr, Mrs, Miss etc)
{MY_FIRSTNAME} Your first name
{MY_LASTNAME} Your last name
{MY_FULLNAME} Your full name
{MY_POSITION} Your position (eg CEO)
{MY_COMPANY} Your company's name
{MY_ADDRESSx} Your address (x=1 to 5)
{MY_PHONEx} Your two phone numbers (x=1 to 2)
{MY_FAXx} Your two fax numbers (x=1 to 2)
{MY_EMAILx} Your five e-mail addresses (x=1 to 5)
{MY_WEBx} Your five web site URLs (x=1 to 5)
{MY_MISCx} Your five miscellaneous data fields (x=1 to 5)

AFFILIATE LINKS
{AFFILIATE_LINK} Your Autoresponse Plus affiliate link
{AFFILIATE_LINK_TEXT} Your Autoresponse Plus affiliate link text

SIGNATURES
{SIGNATURE x} Your three e-mail signatures (x=1 to 3)

ADS
{AD x} Your three advertisements (x=1 to 3)

SUPPORT
{SUPPORT_EMAIL} Your support desk e-mail address

ANTI-SPAM
{ANTISPAM_MESSAGE} Your anti-spam message

The data for the 'My Record' tags is set up by clicking on Your Profile on the menu bar. The data for the affiliate link, signature, ads, support and anti-spam e-mail tags is set up by clicking on Setup on the menu bar.
