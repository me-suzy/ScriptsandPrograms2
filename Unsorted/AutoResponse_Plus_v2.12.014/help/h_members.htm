When you bought Autoresponse Plus, you automcatically received one year's free access to the private upgrade area. We will e-mail you when this is about to expire. You can check the status of your account at any time by visiting
http://www.autoresponseplus.com/accounts

If your account as expired and you would like to renew your membership, please visit
http://www.autoresponseplus.com/order
