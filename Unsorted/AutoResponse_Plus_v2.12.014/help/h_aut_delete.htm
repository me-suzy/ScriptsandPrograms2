DELETING AN AUTORESPONDER

Overview

  1. Click on Autoresponders on the menu bar.
  2. Click on Delete beside the autoresponder you want to delete.
  3. You will be asked to confirm your choice.

Deleting an autoresponder also deletes it's follow-up messages, header, footer and all other information relating to it. Existing follow-up sequences for this autoresponder will remain in the database although they will not continue. The subscriber information will also still be available.

Hint
Only delete an autoresponder when it has no active subscribers, otherwise subscribers will be left wondering why they did not receive the remaining follow-up messages. The best way to do this is to set the 'Status' of the autoresponder to 'Dormant' so that no more subscriptions will be allowed. Then simply wait for all existing follow-up sequences to finish before deleting the autoresponder.
