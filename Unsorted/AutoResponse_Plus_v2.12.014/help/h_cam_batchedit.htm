CHANGING THE STATUS OF MULTIPLE SUBSCRIBERS

Overview

  1. Click on Subscribers on the menu bar.
  2. Set the filter for the subscribers you want to change (or click Clear Filter on the sub menu bar for all subscribers).
  3. Click on Batch Status Change on the sub menu bar.
  4. Choose the new status for the selected subscribers.
  5. Click the Confirm Status Change button.

When you click Batch Status Change, you will see the Subscribers > Batch Status Change screen. Choose the new status for the selected subscribers and click the Confirm Status Change button. The choices for status are:

  • Active
  • Suspended
  • Finished
  • User Cancelled
  • Failed

When you have clicked the Confirm Status Change button, please wait for confirmation that the process is complete before taking any further action. This could take a few minutes for long lists.
