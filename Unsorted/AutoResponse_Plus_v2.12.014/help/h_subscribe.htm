HOW DO PEOPLE SUBSCRIBE TO MY AUTORESPONDERS?

Contacts can subscribe to your autoresponders in five ways:

  • Completing a form on your web page
  • Clicking a mailto link on your web page
  • Clicking a mailto link in your e-mails
  • Clicking a mailto link elsewhere (eg e-zine ads, e-books etc)
  • By sending a blank e-mail to your autoresponder's subscription address
  • Completing a form on your web page

This is the most powerful method as it gives you most opportunity for gathering information from your contact. You can use the automatic code generator to produce the HTML code for you. The generated code contains no formatting information, allowing you to easily blend it in with your web site.

EXAMPLE WEB PAGE SUBSCRIPTION FORM
(not a functioning example)

First name

Last name

E-mail address

Plain text HTML

  • Clicking a mailto link on your web page

This is a good way of obtaining subscriptions from your web site without having to take up valuable real estate on the screen. Simply create a link on your page which, when clicked, opens up a new e-mail addressed to your autoresponder. The link can optionally include a tracking tag as the subject. The automatic code generator can create the HTML for the link. The 'link text' on the automatic code generator screen is what your contact will see as a clickable link.

EXAMPLE WEB PAGE SUBSCRIPTION LINK
(not a functioning example)
If you would like to receive instant information on our Widget product, click here to send us a blank e-mail. You will receive the information within seconds.

Thanks

The Widget Team
  • Clicking a mailto link in your e-mails or elsewhere (eg e-book, e-zine etc)

This works in a similar way to the web page link but does not use HTML code. Again, the link can contain a tracking tag as the subject. The automatic code generator will create the link for you.

EXAMPLE PLAIN TEXT E-ZINE SUBSCRIPTION LINK
(not a functioning example)
************* SPONSOR MESSAGE *************
Is your site selling as much as it should? Click the
link below to INSTANTLY receive 1001 ways to
increase sales.
mailto:info@yourdomain.com?TRA5
********** END OF SPONSOR MESSAGE**********
  • By sending a blank e-mail to your autoresponder's subscription address

Simply give an instruction to your contact, telling them exactly what to do. Your contact can choose the message format they would to receive by typing 'html' or 'plain' in the subject line.

SENDING A BLANK E-MAIL TO YOUR AUTORESPONDER'S SUBSCRIPTION ADDRESS
(not a functioning example)
If you would like to receive more information on our Widget product, simply send a blank e-mail to us at info@yourdomain.com. Type html in the subject line if you want the colour brochure.

Our system will reply within seconds.

Simply choose the subscription method which suits your business. All subscription methods can be used for the same autoresponder.
