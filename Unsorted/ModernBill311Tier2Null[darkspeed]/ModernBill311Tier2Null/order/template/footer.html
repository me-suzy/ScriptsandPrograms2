Sample Order URLS: (You may modify to match YOUR needs!) 


Default order URL: [index.php>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php

Change order form language dynamically: index.php?&set_language=spanish>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?set_language=spanish

Pass any domain from your custom order form (REGISTER): [index.php?submit_register=1&domain=modernbill&tld_extension=com>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?submit_register=1&domain=modernbill&tld_extension=com

Pass any domain from your custom order form (TRANSFER): [index.php?submit_transfer=1&domain=modernbill&tld_extension=com>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?submit_transfer=1&domain=modernbill&tld_extension=com

Order without entering a domain: [index.php?submit_skip=1>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?submit_skip=1

Order via affiliate URL: [index.php?aid=mbadmin>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?aid=123456

Pass in default Package & Plan: [index.php?pack_id=1&pack_plan=3>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?pack_id=1&pack_plan=3 {see notes below}

Clear your cart and start over: [index.php?submit_clear=1>Order Now!]
Syntax: index.php?submit_clear=1

{notes}
pack_id=X where X = The PackageID(pack_id) you want auto-selected.
pack_plan=Y where Y = 1(Monthly) or 3(Quarterly) or 6(Semi-Annually) or 12(Yearly)
If one either X or Y is not correct, nothing will be auto-selected.
