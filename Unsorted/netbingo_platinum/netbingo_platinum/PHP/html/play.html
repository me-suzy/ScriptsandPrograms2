How to Play

Entering the game

  • The display shows when the next game starts and how many players are already enrolled to play. The display is automatically refreshed every 10 seconds.
  • Click the Draw Cards to receive your bingo cards.
  • You will be automatically dealt five cards.
  • The countdown will continue until it is time for the game to start
  • If you are the only player enrolled at gametime, then you will play against the computer. To give you a sporting chance, the computer cannot win until at least 35 balls have been drawn.
  • If you missed the start of the game because your cards were still downloading, you will have to draw your cards again to join the next game.
  • While waiting for the game to start, you can "chat" with other players by clicking the "chat" link below the NetBingo logo.

Playing the Game

  • Every 10 seconds the game announces a new ball. The current ball is displayed in the center of the top frame.The most recent five balls are displayed below the current ball and the "lightboard" shows all the balls called in the game so far.
  • Mark the numbers on your card as the balls are called. when you have 5 adjacent numbers marked horizontally, vertically or diagonally, push the I Won! BINGO I Won! button.
  • If you really won, the game will stop, and tell you that you have won. If not, the current game continues until somebody wins, or until the balls run out.
  • Dont push the I Won! BINGO I Won! button too often! If you "cry wolf" three times, you're out of the game, and may lose some credits.
