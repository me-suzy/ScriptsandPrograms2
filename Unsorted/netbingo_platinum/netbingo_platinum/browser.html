Browser Notes

Our game is designed to be played in a browser dimensioned for 800 x 600 display or greater.

So that you are not waiting for the browser to download the drawn ball image, or download each of the card cell images, we download these images at the start of the game and store them in your browser's memory.

Because of the quantity of graphics used in the game - 75 ball images and 2 x 75 card cell images - the memory requirements are high and we recommend that you close all other programs while playing the game. If insufficient resources are available in your browser, the game may lock-up. If this happens, click the "click to refresh" link in the top right hand corner. Do not click your browser's refresh or reload button - this may put you back to the start of the game.

The game has been tested under Internet Explorer 5.0 and Netscape Communicator 4.74 on the Windows 98 SE platform. The game makes extensive use of javascript and is not compatible with Opera 4.02 or earlier browsers.
