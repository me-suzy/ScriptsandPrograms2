Send Newsletter

The Send Newsletter feature allows you to send any saved newsletter template to your mailing list. A list of saved newsletter templates is presented with the option to Preview & Send next to each template. The send newsletter function is a manual process but could be easily automated with the use of automation tools such as Windows Task Scheduler.

Preview & Send

The Preview & Send link allows you to preview your newsletter before it is sent to your mailing list. You are given the opportunity to modify the newsletter template on the preview page if you wish to make changes. Once you are happy with your newsletter layout and content you can send the newsletter using the "Send this Newsletter" button provided at the bottom of the newsletter preview page.

The generated newsletter includes each subscriber's email address within the blind carbon copy address, ensuring email addresses are kept confidential.
