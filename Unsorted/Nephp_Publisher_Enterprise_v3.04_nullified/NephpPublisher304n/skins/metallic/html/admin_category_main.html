This is category manager. With this tool, you can add new categories. The categories are fully customizable. In this edition, you can obtain unlimited number of categories. It structures in such a way that optimize for speed and accuracy.

1. To add root category

   + To add a root category, you just simply click on the "add root directory on your left right hand. And then feel in all the required fields.


2. To add a sub-category

   + All you need to you is to click on the category tree on the left-hand side. You will see its properties. Then, you click on "Add sub". There you go, you have a sub category belong to the category that you clicked on.
