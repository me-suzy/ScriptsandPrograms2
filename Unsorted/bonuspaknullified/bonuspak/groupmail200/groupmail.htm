WELCOME

GroupMail provides a complete email service to anyone having access to a browser which can connect to the internet. Since many institutions such as schools and libraries provide free public access to the internet, it is not necessary to purchase a dial-up or cable account from an ISP in order to use email.

GroupMail is a full-featured email client which can accomodate many users simultaneously. Each user is allocated a block of file storage space to hold personal files, mail folders, address book etc. The user interface is friendly and intuitive and includes a modest help facility.

GroupMail is ideal for people who are connected in some manner e.g. family, school, social or church group, business or government office, K-12 children etc. Users can be made known to one another through a posted member list and can communicate via an Intercom facility which is separate from the email service. Intercom messages are deposited immediately into members' inboxes and never go out to the internet. Sending and receiving attachments is supported. Register or login below.
