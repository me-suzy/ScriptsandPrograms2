Info:
  100janCMS Articles Control is a complete php/mysql based articles administration
  application, including articles management, commenting and rating articles by visitors.

  Features:

   Advanced back-end articles management system.
   Displaying articles by unique marker.
   Multiple language support (html encoding).
   Advanced WYSIWYG editor to accept html content with images upload.
   Articles visits count (on full text page).
   Rating articles by visitors.
   Commenting articles by visitors.
   Registering visitors.
   Supports multiple users with custom access levels.
   Open source application, for easy modifications.
   Commented php source code, for easy integration and adaption.
   Help & documentation.
