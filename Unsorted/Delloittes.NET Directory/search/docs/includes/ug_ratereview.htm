Rate & Review

The "Rate & Review" feature allows visitors to comment and rate individual resources within your directory. Provided next to each resource within the directory is the count of current reviews and a link allowing users to Rate & Review a specific item.

If a user chooses to rate or review a resource they are ask to provide their full name, an email or web address, their comments with regards to the resource and a 1-5 star rating (5 being the highest).
