Securing Contenido

Author Timo A. Hummel
Created 13th November 2003
Audience
 Site Administrators
Applies to
 Contenido 4.4 or later

Introduction

By default, Contenido is delivered with the "admin" and "sysadmin" accounts. There's nothing wrong with that, but just a littlebit "insecure".

What to do

The first thing you should introduce are "personal accounts". Just create an own account for everybody who works with Contenido. There's also no need to apply rights to them; you can do that via the groups.

Group magic

If you are done with the personal accounts, you can go on to the groups. We recommend the following groups:

Group Name
 Description
sysadmins
 System Administrators
admins
 Client Administrators (if required)
editors
 People who are maintaining content

You can then put the individual people into several groups.

Advantages

You gain a few advantages by using that concept:

- If people leave your organisation, you just have to remove their individual accounts
- You don't have to change a password and communicate it to everybody
- You can apply rights to the group, so you dont have to apply rights individually
