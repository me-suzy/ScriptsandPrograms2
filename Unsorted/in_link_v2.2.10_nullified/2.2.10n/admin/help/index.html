Help

This is the administration utility. All of the functions of In-Link are controlled from here. You can navigate the directories normally and add, delete, or edit any categories or links. You can also use the search feature to find specific categories or links that you want delete or edit. Deleting of a category will result in the removal of all sub-categories and links. All the fields of a link or a category can be edited including the hits, rating, votes, and date created.
