About: Arkeia is the solution for automated backup/archiving and restoration on Linux, UNIX, and Windows NT networks. ARKEIA utilizes an exclusive multi-flow technology to deliver speeds that are 200 to 300% faster than rival software packages. Its unique transaction engine allows multiple backups and restores to be performed simultaneously with total reliability. This is especially valuable for multiple users backing up or restoring data on their own. (A multiple drive library is required).
