Organization Information

  1. What is your mission statement?

  2. Please describe two examples of programs or projects completed in the last year. If your organization is new, please describe the research you have done substantiating the need for your program.

  3. What is the geographic area you serve?

  4. What will be accomplished with this grant? Who and how many people will benefit?

  5. What is the amount of money you are requesting and how will it be spent? Provide specific project, program, budget details. What is the minimum amount needed and maximum amount sought?

  6. If we are unable to fully fund your proposal, are there specific components that could be partially funded? Explain.

  7. Name other sources of funding, including dollar amounts you have received within the last two years.

  8. What are the program or project's expected outcomes? How will the success of these outcomes be measured? What is the timeline for implementation of this program or project? (Note: You should have measurable results within one year)

  9. How did you hear about the eBay Foundation? Are there any eBay employees or eBay registered users who are active with your organization?

Document Checklist

In addition to emailing the Grant Application to foundation@ebay.com, please also mail the following to the eBay Foundation:

  • A hardcopy of the Grant Application
  • Proof of nonprofit status (a copy of IRS 501(c)3 letter)
  • Itemized budget for the project, including funds that have already been committed
  • A current audited financial statement and a detailed budget for the organization
  • A copy of the organization's nondiscrimination policy

I certify that all of the above listed information is included in this proposal statement. Incomplete proposals may not be considered.
