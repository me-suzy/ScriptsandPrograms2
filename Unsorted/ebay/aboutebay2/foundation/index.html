Welcome to the eBay Foundation


Here is what we are all about:


  • We reflect the values and community orientation of eBay.
  • We provide tools, hope, and direction to those who seek new skills. We teach the teachers, who in turn help people recognize the potential in themselves to improve their surroundings, their lives, and their communities.
  • We are like eBay -- clever, unique, passionate, and eclectic. Our programs have long-term implications and maximize the ability of the Foundation to do good in the world.
