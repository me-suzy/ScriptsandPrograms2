Compensation & Benefits Manager

Reporting directly to the Vice President of Human Resources, the Compensation and Benefits Manager will provide the strategic direction for all corporate compensation and benefits programs.

Compensation responsibilities will include continued development of job matrixes, salary and stock option structures; executive compensation; performance management; bonus plan design and administration to insure the achievement of equitable and competitive employee compensation. Collaborate with managers and the HR team to implement changes that are a cultural fit. Develop and improve tools for managers and employees which enable them to understand their goals, objectives and metrics; build a development plan; and allow for timely and clear performance feedback.

Benefits responsibilities will include the management and administration of all existing benefit programs, evaluation and addition of new/improved program enhancements, negotiation of costs and services, development of policies and procedures and the directing of the annual company-wide open enrollment process. Education and/or Relevant Experience:

  • 8+ years of progressive experience in Compensation & benefits in a high-tech environemnt, with a minimum of 2 years in a management role.
  • BS/BA degree in HR or a related field (or equivalent experience)
  • A demonstrated ability to meet organizational needs through the planning and implementation of key initiatives in both areas is a must.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of all federal and state laws influencing both areas.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
