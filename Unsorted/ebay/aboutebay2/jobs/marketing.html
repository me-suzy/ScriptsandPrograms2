Graphic Artist (99-052)

This is a great opportunity for a highly imaginative, enthusiastic, organized person. We are looking for a talented, creative individual who is a proven self-starter and motivated to oversee all creative aspects of a major website. Must be able to juggle multiple projects internally and freelancer and creative ad agency activities externally. Outstanding written/oral communication skills and the ability to work with marketing and engineering departments and understand/interpret their needs. You must have strong interpersonal skills, team-player attitude, and a keen sense of humor. Versatile, capable, and productive designer who understand both design and implementation processes. These skills should be clearly demonstrated in the submission of at least 3 URL samples. Please include a brief review of the processes, techniques, and your specific role in the projects.

Qualifications/Skills Required:
HTML digital design, page layout, Photoshop, thorough knowledge of color, and typography. Helpful if you have working knowledge of Illustrator, XPress, Pagemaker, and Debabelizer in PC and Mac formats. This opening is for an immediate full-time salaried position.

To be considered, you must have a minimum of 1.5 - 2 years experience in new media design with a strong web background. A degree in graphic design is strongly preferred

Major Account Manager

The Account Manager will nurture, oversee and manage strategic relationships with eBay's top sellers and dealers. The position will have input and help define special programs like "The Premium Sellers Club". They will review link requests, assess partner viability and have daily contact with our top sellers. The position will be the primary point of contact for dealers, will handle conflict resolution, participate in trade events and working in a cross-functional team, support business development efforts. They should be able to convert complaints into customers. They should be able to establish rapport, be diplomatic, solutions driven, implement dealer surveys and rapidly grow the customer dealer base by size and revenue. The primary responsibility will be to grow our dealer base and enhance revenue. The position will handle dealer needs assessment and source information regarding dealer interest and activities, and make recommendations to a Business Development Manager. The position will chaperone common customer issues in conjunction with the Customer Support team.

Education and/or Relevant Experience
3-5 years of Account Management experience required. Education and/or Relevant Experience: The candidate should have a BS in business or equivalent work experience, prior Web experience and a working fluency in PowerPoint, Excel, etc.

Director, Business Development

The Director of Business Development, reporting to the Director of Internet Marketing and Business Development, will be responsible for identifying and developing relationships, and negotiating agreements with key vertical and horizontal partners. These include online and traditional publishers of collectibles related content and information. This individual will also be responsible for developing similar relationships with transaction partners who help facilitate trading and consummate transactions between users include technology providers, financial institutions, shippers and escrow companies. In addition to developing and managing external relationships, this individual will work closely with the vertical programs, product development and trade show teams in identifying and implementing the appropriate content and transaction partnerships. Overtime, the individual may be responsible for building and managing a team I to 2 individuals.

Education and/or Relevant Experience
The successful candidate should have 3 to 5 years' experience identifying, developing and negotiating agreements in a competitive marketplace. Demonstrated skills in market segmentation and customer identification, and competitive analysis are important. The successful candidate will be familiar with the marketing strategy of the major Internet companies and have a strong understanding of the various Internet revenue and partnership models. The candidate should also be familiar with the collectibles market including the consumer profile and the type of content and information these individuals deem important. The strongest candidate will also have demonstrated leadership skills, a proven track record of team building and 1 to 2 years' Internet experience. Although not essential, an MBA is preferred.

European Marketing Manager

Job Summary:
The Region Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and implementing marketing programs that support the strategy of the region marketing plan. The programs include both real world as well as Internet-based (on-site) components, and must include grass roots (customer-driven) elements also.

The Region Marketing Manager will handle multiple projects in a fast-paced and rapidly growing environment. They will oversee all stages of program implementation, including performance measurement and budgeting, and collaborate with the US-based International Marketing and Product Managers as well as the Region Director to ensure that the programs fit into the overall strategy for the company. Most importantly, the Region Marketing Manager must be able to move quickly and be flexible.

Over time, the Marketing Manager is expected to develop a strong understanding and deep knowledge of the business in their country territories. This includes a solid understanding of the customer segments and cultural specifics that influence customer behavior in the target countries.

The Region Marketing Manager will be evaluated on the following:

  • Ability to develop customer base
  • Managing multiple projects simultaneously
  • Working with cross functional teams both in Europe and in US
  • Completing projects on time and within budget
  • Managing vendor relationships to accomplish goals (PR, marketing communications, etc.)
  • In depth knowledge of customer segments and behavior
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Resourceful
  • Action oriented; likes to get out and get things done
  • Good presentation skills

We are looking for two managers to handle marketing activities in the United Kingdom, Scandinavia and Germany. One manager will be based in the United Kingdom, probably London, and the other will be based in Holland, probably Amsterdam. Both managers will report to a European Director, based in Holland.

Education and/or Relevant Experience:

  • BA or BS degree required; MBA preferred
  • Relevant consumer marketing experience at a European Internet commerce company
  • See evaluation criteria for additional information.
