BS/BA and 1-2 years of project coordination experience. Must have experience with Microsoft Project, Excel, and other project management tools and techniques. Must have knowledge of the software product development cycle.

User-Interaction Designer

Job Description/Areas of Responsibility
Do you have a passion for user-interaction design? Help design, prototype and test the next generation user interfaces for eBay. You will help design and build user interfaces that make eBay as easy, fun and efficient as possible for its millions of users. You will work closely with many different teams at eBay, including those from product development, product marketing and customer support.

Primary responsibilities: 1. Develop scenarios illustrating users; interaction with eBay; 2. Create user-interface prototypes suitable for usability testing; 3. Make usability assessments for existing and future features; 4. Design simple solutions for known usability breakdowns; 5. Explore new user-interface paradigms for online commerce.

Education and/or Relevant Experience:
Must be skilled in client-side web development (HTML, Javascript). Must be proficient with rapid prototyping tools such as director, Photoshop, VB, Perl, Tcl, etc. must be able to demonstrate strong user-interaction design skills and familiarity with usability engineering methodologies.

Must have at least 3 years of user-interface design work. Interested candidates should bring a portfolio of past work.

User Interface Engineer

Job Description/Areas of Responsibility
Want to have your code be experienced by millions of users? Help design and implement new user-interface features on eBay's popular website. As a member of eBay's product development group, you will interact with product marketing and customer support to create next-generation user interfaces for online commerce.

Primary responsibilities: 1. Implement end-user features in C++; 2. Design user interactions; 3. Implement front-end user interfaces in HTML and Javascript.

Secondary responsibilities: 1. Create paper and electronic prototypes for testing; 2. Uncover usability problems.

Education and/or Relevant Experience:
At least 3 years of C++ experience. Must be proficient in client-side web development - HTML is a must, as well as CGI or ISAPI experience; Javascript knowledge is a plus. Familiarity with usability engineering methodologies is a plus, but not required. Working knowledge of SQL is also a plus.

Sr. Change Management Engineer

Reporting to the Director of Quality Assurance and Change Management, this position is responsible for ensuring stable, controlled migration of hardware, software, and networking changes into the eBay Internet site production environment by working closely with members of Engineering, Operations, System Administration, Customer Support, and QA to coordinate and implement changes. This position is responsible for assessing and managing the risk of change in order to ensure quality service and customer satisfaction, as well as for planning, managing and implementing the eBay application release process. The position is also responsible for maintaining consistency between the production and test environments, as well as migrating application changes into and maintaining the test environment. The position is responsible for participating in regular change review meetings, managing regular application rollout meetings, as well as keeping abreast of industry trends and changes in software and hardware technologies.

Education and/or Relevant Experience:
Competency in Unix, NT, SUN hardware, and problem analysis required. Familiarity with managing change in a complex, networked client/server and INTERNET computing environments. Experience in configuration management, release management or quality assurance is a plus. Must be customer oriented and must be proactive in ensuring stable and continuous computing services required by eBay. This position also requires good, written, and oral communication skills. Must be organized, goal oriented, and a self-starter. Q.A. Engineer

Job Summary:
Participates in the evolution and execution of procedures for ensuring the quality of new features and releases of the software supporting eBay's Personal Trading Community. This includes, but is not limited to, reporting defects, developing test plans and test cases, as well as executing black box and automated regression tests. Participates in the ongoing management and maintenance of the defect and test case databases, participates in defect tracking and trend measurement processes, tracks and manages defects reported in production.

Responsibilities:
Works with Operations and Product Development to maintain Alpha, Beta, and Live-to-Site entry criteria. Works with Change Management to review the effectiveness of QA in maintaining product quality. Works with the Engineering and Performance Management teams to develop test plans and test cases. Works with the Director of Quality to assess and help plan test effort required for new functions/features under development.

Education and/or Relevant Experience:
3-5 years experience in Quality Assurance, including writing test plans and test cases, as well as conducting black box and automated testing. Must have participated in the QA and release of at least two production products and/or releases. Demonstrated communications skills, as well as the ability to work well with other departments in reaching quality goals along with live-to-site delivery deadlines.
