FAQ

When did eBay go public and at what price?
eBay went public in September 1998 and the IPO price was $18 a share.

How long has eBay been in business?
eBay was launched in September 1995. The first auction was conducted on Labor Day, and within a few weeks, buyers and sellers began flocking to the service.

What is eBay's Fiscal Year-end?
eBay's fiscal year-end is December 31. Reporting quarters correspond with calendar quarters.

Does eBay have a direct purchase plan?
No, eBay does not have a direct purchase plan.

Does eBay pay a cash dividend?
No, eBay has never declared a cash dividend and presently intends to continue this policy.

How many employees does eBay have?
Currently, eBay has approximately 130 employees.
