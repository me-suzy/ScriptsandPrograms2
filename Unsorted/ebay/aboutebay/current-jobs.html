Strategic Analyst

The Strategic Analyst will initiate, manage and analyze company metrics, and will disseminate information accordingly throughout the organization. Supporting the Strategic Planning and Analysis department, the Strategic Analyst will play a key, ongoing role in the evaluation of the numeric trends in the business. The Strategic Analyst will also be engaged in project work that will support the investigation of new lines of business, mergers and acquisitions, and competitive analysis.

The successful candidate for this role will have excellent quantitative skills. The successful applicant will not only be able to glean insights from quantitative data, but will also be able to communicate these insights effectively. Excellent interpersonal skills will be expected.

This position will report to the VP of Strategic Planning and Analysis.

Education and/or Relevant Experience: This position will require a technical undergraduate degree and 3+ years of relevant experience. Experience in the professional services industry, especially Management Consulting or Investment Banking and/or an MBA are not required but would be considered an asset.
