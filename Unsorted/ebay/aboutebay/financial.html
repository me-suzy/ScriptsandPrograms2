 eBay is the world's personal trading community. eBay created a new market--efficient one-to-one trading on the web. eBay facilitates trade between people in an auction format.

Founded in September 1995, eBay has experienced tremendous success. eBay has grown consistently at 20-30% per month and has been profitable since the beginning. eBay is the leader in its market with an 89% market share. In Q1 1998, eBay users transacted over $100 million in gross merchandise sales on eBay.

In March 1998, eBay ranked third in the overall number of minutes spend on an Internet site following only Yahoo! and AOL.com. eBay is ranked first among the most used commerce sites on the Internet ahead of well know brands like Amazon.com, ColumbiaHouse.com, CDNow.com and Barnes&Noble.com

Today, eBay's 750,000 registered users conduct over 375,000 simultaneous auctions on any given day. They start over 60,000 new auctions a day and bid 225,000 times daily. To date, nearly 11 million auctions have taken place on eBay!
