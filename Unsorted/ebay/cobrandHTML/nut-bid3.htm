Searching for Stuff

The second way to find items is to use the automatic search facility. Search has a few advantages over browsing. Search is:

  • Simple
  • Fast
  • Easy to use
  • Powerful
  • Flexible

By learning how to use the search engine, you can locate exactly what you are looking for in record time.

To get to the search page, click on "Search" at the top or bottom of any eBay page. If we fill in the words "antique doll," here is what the search screen would look like:

Searching is easy.

The only field you need to fill in is the "Search for title" field. All the other fields on the screen are optional and are used for more advanced searches.

To see the real, "live" version of the search form, click here.

Once we press the "Go!" button, we will see the results of the search (not shown here in the Tutorial).
