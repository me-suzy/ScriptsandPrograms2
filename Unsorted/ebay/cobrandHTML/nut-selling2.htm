Verifying Your Entry

On the previous screen, we entered the auction information about our rare collection of beanie babies. After hitting the "review" button, we are brought to this screen to allow us to verify the auction details.

You will now be shown what you have listed for sale at the auction. You will also be shown the cost to you, the seller. Finally, there is great deal of information and disclaimers about the auction process.

We have included some of the fine print here for your benefit. But it's always good to keep up-to-date with the online version.

You Have Verified Your Listing.

You now know the information is correct, and you know what your charges will be. Once you click on the "I agree to these terms and fees" button, a confirmation screen will appear. Let's move on to the next screen to see what the Selling Confirmation looks like.
