Registration: Step 2 of 2
Confirm your registration.

Next, you will receive an email from eBay with a special, temporary password. You will be asked to confirm your registration and select a new password.

Follow the directions in the email.

Confirm your registration using your temporary password.

Using the temporary password you received from eBay by email (75845 in this example), confirm your registration and select your new password. The new password will be the password you use for future transactions on eBay.

You can discard the special eBay-supplied password (75845 in this example), as you will not need it again.

Remember, don't give out your new password to others.

That's all there is to it!

Now you can bid, you can conduct your own auctions, and you can leave feedback about your experiences. You are part of the eBay community.
