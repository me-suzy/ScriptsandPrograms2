Selling: Step 1 of 3
Starting Your Auction - List Your Item For Sale

You are now a registered user, you've seen that others in the community have listed their items for sale, and you have something that you'd like to put up for auction, like a collection of 100 beanie babies.

To get to the "List a New Item" page, click on the "List an item for sale" graphic on the homepage.

Here are 4 easy-to-follow steps that you need to think about.

Step 1. Create a short  title for your item.

Step 2. Select a category to list your item in.

Step 3. Write a compelling description for your item.

Step 4. Decide on the lowest acceptable price you'd like to get.

You may also think about some advanced options for your auction, such as a picture URL, a boldface title, specifying an optional reserve price, a private auction, etc.

Once you've prepared your listing, you're ready to begin. This sample screen has been simplified from the online version and some of the legal wording removed. Remember, you are entering into an agreement with eBay and your potential customers.
