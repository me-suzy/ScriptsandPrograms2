Congratulations! You have now learned how to list an item for sale at eBay.

Note that the listings pages are updated hourly, so your new item will not appear in the listings pages immediately.

Hint: If you want to tell your family and friends about the item you are selling on \$HOMENAME, or would like to advertise your item on bulletin boards or newsgroups, the direct URL (web address) to your item would be

<http://iguana.ebay.com/aw-cgi/ebayISAPI?ViewItem&item=40>.
Of course, you'll need to replace "40" with the item number for your particular item.
