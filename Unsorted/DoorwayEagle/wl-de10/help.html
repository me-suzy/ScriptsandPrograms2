What is "Choose search engine to target"?
Here you should choose which search engine you are wanting to get a good ranking in, by clicking the radio button to the left of the search engine you are targeting. When you choose a search engine, DoorwayEagle will create a doorway page spefically for good placement in the search engine you selected. For best results, no matter the format you choose when you create a doorway, it should be submitted to all search engines, not just the one you are targeting. Although some search engines no longer have a free submission service, many still do.

What is the "Title of new doorway page"?
The Title of the doorway page is an important part of the doorway page creation process. In search engines, this will show up as the clickable link when your listing comes up for a search term. It is recommended that your keywords (or keyword phrases) exist here, but you also want to write a Title that visitors to the search engine will want to click on after they've read your listing. If you can add as many keywords to your Title as possible, at the same time as making potential visitors WANT to click on your link, you're on your way to maximizing your search engine traffic! Be sure to keep the title of the page short, no more than about 10 words in length.

More information on the "Keywords" field.
Having the right keywords in your doorway page is almost as important as having a good page title. A Keyword can either be a single word like "collectibles", or a phrase like "vintage collectibles". Each keyword or keyword phrase should be separated by a comma. It is recommended that you have no more than 3 keywords for each doorway page.

What is the "Description of page"?
The description of a doorway page should also contain all of your keywords for best placement results, and should be no more than two sentences long. This field is very simple, just describe the product or service you are making this doorway page for. This will also show up on many of the search engine listings right under the clickable link which leads from the search engine to your page. If possible, write the description of your page so as to catch your potential visitor's attention and make them want to visit your site!

What is the "Text to be displayed on page"?
The "Text you would like to be displayed on page" field pretty much says it all. Here, you will want to type the text that will be displayed right in the middle of the doorway page when it's finished. It is not necessary to add all of your keywords in this field, but adding a few wouldn't hurt. This text can be as long as you'd like it to be.

What is the "URL of page you're linking to"?
In this field, you will enter the actual URL or internet address you are creating this doorway page for. So if you want to direct traffic from this doorway page to your homepage, you would enter http://yourhomepage.com in this field (or whatever "your homepage" address is).

What is the "Link to your main page"?
The Link to your main page is the URL of your MAIN website. On the doorway page, a link to this address will be created towards the bottom of the page. This can provide better "Link Popularity" to your main site in search engines, and can help your site's ranking in the future.

What is "Your website's logo URL"?
The Logo URL is simply the URL, or location, of your main website's logo. For example: http://domaineagle.com/images/logo.gif. If you do not have a company logo, or it is not online at the moment, just enter the address to any image on your website in this field.

What is "File name of new doorway"?
This is what your doorway page will be named on your server when you have finished creating it. Be sure to end the name with either .htm, or .html. Try to avoid using blank spaces in the name, use an underscore in the place of blank spaces instead. Do not use slashes, or any special characters besides the dash "-", and underscore "_" as mentioned above. Otherwise ONLY use numbers and letters for naming the page. It's normally a good idea to put one or more keywords in the name of your page as well. Separate your keywords by a dash - For example: vintage-collectibles.html

What is "Add javascript redirect to page"?
If you check the box next to "Add javascript redirect to page", a special javascript will be inserted into the heading of your doorway page which will automatically redirect all traffic from the doorway page, to the "URL of the page you're linking to". This eliminates the need for the visitor to manually click on the link to go to the page you're directing them to.

I've created a doorway page, now what?
You need to submit your new doorway page to search engines! Although some search engines have started charging for all new listings (like Excite and their network), many are still FREE. Listed below are some of the most popular ones that still have a free submission service. The URL to your doorway page is provided to you right after you create it, copy this URL (select it, and press Ctrl + C) and visit each of the following search engines and submit it by pasting in the address of your doorway page in the URL field:

All The Web, AltaVista, AOL Search, DirectHit, Google, HotBot, Lycos
