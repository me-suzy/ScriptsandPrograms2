Need Help On Posting An Item?

Title/Item Name:
Put in the name of the item that you are selling.  (17" Sony Monitor, Rare English Book etc.)

Category:
You must have a category to put your auction site in.

Image URL:
If you do not have an image that you can link to online be sure to erase the http:// in the form field.  If you do have an imaage you would like to put on your auction enter the full url in this form field.

Days Until Close:
How long you want your auction to last 1 day to 14 days long.

Item Condition:
The condition of the item is good for others to know.

Shipping Method:
Enter the only method that YOU will shop the item to them.

Payment Method(s):
Click each method of payment that you will accept.

Description:
Self explanatory.  You may also use html tags.

Your Handle/Alias:
Must enter this in to track your post.

Password:
Must enter this in to track your post.

Your Starting Bid:
This is what you would like to start the auction price out at.  Do not use the $ sign.

Your Reserved Price:
This means you will not sell the item no less than.  This item field must be larger than the starting bid price. Do not use the $ sign.

Buy It Now Price:
This means that a person can buy the item you are selling at a price that you fit.  This must be larger than the starting bid price.  Do not use the $ sign.

Bid Incriment:
Enter in if you want people to bid .25 or .50 or 1.00 per bid.
