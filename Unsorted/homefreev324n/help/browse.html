Account Browsing
Posted on: February 28, 2000, 1:22 a.m.

Browsing and searching accounts from admin section of Home Free

Simply login to your admin.cgi script and click the Browse Feature.

Advanced Browsing Option:

  • Accounts in category: Allows you to view accounts in selected directories or all directories. Moderators will only have the option to view accounts in categorizes they are moderators in. To select more then one category, hold down the Control Key while clicking the categorizes. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

  • Show accounts joining: allows you to view accounts that signed up on/before or after a certain date. This can be useful for finding accounts that are new or old or that you think were not around when you added a certain feature and you want to make sure. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

  • Show only accounts taking up allows you to view accounts based on how much space they take up which can be helpful for finding sites using your service for high volume sites, file mirroring or finding accounts without much activity and for finding just who is using up your space. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

  • Accounts using: is extremely useful for finding sites that have a great deal of files or sites that have very few files and therefore may either not be used often or may be mirroring files on your site. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

  • Sort matching accounts by: allows you to view accounts by several options to find whatever select group of sites you want. This can be very helpful for find sites that are rarely used or sites that are hosting illegal file types or such. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

  • # Accounts per page: allows you to select the number of accounts to view on the results page. The lower the number, the faster the results will load but the longer it will take to browse through all the options. Click the "Retrieve Matching Accounts" button when you are done.

Once you have searched for a certain type of account, you will see all the matches (up to the selected number) on a results page.

You will have the ability to put an any match on hold, take an account of hold, view files in an account, delete an account and view many other important bits of information on a given account. This makes finding certain account types easy and simplifies the viewing process. Only admins can delete accounts.

If you would like to view more information on account, you can run it through the View Account admin option, however many of the important viewing options are already printed in the search results. In the event that you make any account changes, click the Delete/Update Selected Accounts button to save and process the results.
