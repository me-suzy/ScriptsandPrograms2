Welcome to the 50/50 Trade Association. In this section we'll describe the services and benefits of our trade organization.
 

We work on the principle of 50/50: 50% trade and 50% cash for all transactions between 50/50 Trade members. We open a new market and offer you more business while allowing you to still cover your costs of most projects and expenses, while gaining trade dollars to use on a project or service for your company or for a purchase of merchandise.

The use of trade dollars can reduce your "out-of-pocket" expense by 50% while not reducing the sales of the company you are working with. Both sides win in this situation.
 
What are "Trade Dollars"?
Trade dollars are monetary units received and spent amongst trade association members for products and services just like real U.S. currency. For every project or service a company provides, they give trade dollars to the company that preforms the service or sells the product. That company, in turn, uses the trade dollars they recieve for a service or product they need. The cycle continues on.
Trade dollars have no expiration date and remain in your account as long as you are a member and can be used at any time, with another member, to reduce your cash expenses by 50%.
 
 
About Our Website:
Our web site was created for the conveinence of our members. We put a collection of helpful functions on our web site to make your experience with 50/50 Trade easy and productive. Listed below is a description of all the different areas of our web site and what they are for.
This section allows for you to utilize your trade dollar account. It offers most of the features as your online banking system at your bank.
With it you can:
  • View your Trade Dollar account's balance
  • Send payments for services provided
  • Request payments from companies looking to seeking work from you
  • Revieve payments form companies that you have provided service to
  • Edit the information about your company
  • Get detailed information about all transaction to and from you account for tracking
  • Provide extra security for your account and the ability to lock you account incase of fraud or if you are going to be out of the area and want the account put on hold.
 
Our bidding section allows you to, both, bid on projects from other companies or create projects for other members of 50/50 Trade Association to bid on for you. It removed the need to manually find companies and submit individual requests for bids from companies.
Each company that signs up can choose different specialities that their company preforms. When a new project is created, each company in that field is notified by E-mail that there is a project to bid on. In turn, they can log in and read the information and specifications about that project and then bid.
Each company submitting a project to bid on can specifiy a detailed description of what the project entails, the maximum budget for the project, and time frame it which the project needs to be done.
For each company providing a bid for the project, they will recieive a notification in their email stating that a project was submitting fitting their classification. They can log on, view the details, the time frame, and the budget, and they submit their quote.
Then the company recieving the bids can read more information about the company bidding, read a description of what can do, see the estimated timeframe, and how much the projects cost is. While similar to a reverse auction, the lowest price is not necessarily the winner. Each company has the option of exploring which ever companies they wish. We provide a way to see a number of quotes and let you decide which is best for your company.
 
Our members sections lists all the businesses that are currently in the Association. It is updated every time a business signs up so you have a new detailed information source that is always changing. We break down all the businesses by their catagory. Simply choose a catagory of the business you are interested in and they choose a company to view.
Also, we are not hiding our members list. Please feel free to check it out.
