keycode script

Great way to have proof and security to download your ebooks, software downloads.
scripts functions -

assign a keycode automatically
password protected admin form
sends email to customers with the keycode info
sends customer another email thanking them after they downloaded.
Creates a database file with customers proof of downloading:
Their IP address
Date & Time of download
Customers Name
Customers email address
URL of where product was downloaded
Keycode that was assigned to them

You can easily change the look of the login page to match your sites
