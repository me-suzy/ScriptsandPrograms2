Usage Guide

Viewing keyword advertising

By selecting "view advertising" on the main management page, all search keyword advertising will be displayed. At the bottom of this page there is a form with which you can add new keyword advertising. Each keyword a banner is to be displayed for must be separated by ','. There must also be no spaces between the ',' and the word, and there must be a ',' at the beginning and end (e.g. ",search,keywords,here,"). You can also add advertising to key phrases (e.g. ",search,key phrase,here,"). Keyword advertising provides HIGH QUALITY impressions of the advertising, greatly increasing click-through's.
