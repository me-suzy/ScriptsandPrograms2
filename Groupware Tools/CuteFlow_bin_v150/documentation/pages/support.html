Support

Free support

You can get free support on the Cuteflow Sourceforge project page (http://sourceforge.net/projects/cuteflow). There are public forums and mailing lists. If you found bugs, you can post them there (BugTracker)

Professional support

If you need professional support (maybe for installation, initial setup of document templates or customized features that are not included in CuteFlow) you can contact the EMEDIA OFFICE GmbH, a company located in Germany (Mail: cuteflow@emedia-office.de, http://www.emedia-office.de).

Please note that this professional support is NOT FREE!
