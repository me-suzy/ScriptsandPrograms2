Install:
---------

Unzip phpcounter_v1.9.zip file.

In 'config.php' add/change 
MySQL database conecting name, password, host.
Change default text/character set your lang.

Use the phpmyadmin and upload(dump) 'sql.sql' file.

Upload (FTP) 'counter.php' and 'config.php'
files.

Copy and paste this line in your page:

/* Counter [ */

include 'counter.php';

/* ] Counter */
