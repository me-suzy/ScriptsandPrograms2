1.4 Released March 13, 2004
1. Homework assignments not published will not be shown online.

7. When people request an account an e-mail is sent to the Digital Scribe Administrator reminding them to login and approve or deny the request.
8. When you view an image from the teacher's description of student work the link back to the description goes back instead of moving you to the first piece of student work.
9. When a teacher edits a homework assignment the due date no longer changes to July.
10. All e-mails sent by the DIgital Scribe have additional header information to help them pass through spam filters.
11. All leftover font tags have been removed. Fonts are now fully controlled through one CSS file.
12. Magic Quotes are no longer required to be turned on in order to use the Digital Scribe.
